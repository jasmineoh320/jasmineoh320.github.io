Stella & Jamie Internship

At Stella & Jamie, I learned the ins and outs of the fashion industry. Although Stella & Jamie is well known amongst A-list celebrity clients, it is still a small, privately own company, so I had a lot of tasks and responsibilities as the only graphic design intern. Not only did I do all the email blasts and any kind of social media campaigns, I helped out with photo shoots for Spring 2015 collection lookbooks. After assisting with photo shoots, I helped refine and polish the images to look professional and stylistic to parallel the concept of Stella & Jamie. It was one of the only internship I've had the experience to have so much responsibility and power as a graphic design intern, but it was very rewardingly challenging and one of the greatest experience to learn the behind-the-scene aspects of the fashion company.
