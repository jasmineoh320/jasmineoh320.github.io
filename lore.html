Lore is a collaborative project that brands clothing with a concept of storytelling. Lore is a conceptual brand that is grown out of a tradition of storytelling, interested in the shared memories of people across generation, gender, and geography. Held to be entirely handmade in Los Angeles, the "417 army" is the first release as part of the new spring collection. The jacket works to combine a diachotomy of young and old, refined and raw, muted and vibrant tones in the goal that pieces will be shared and share from friend to friend, encouraging everyone to be the story that others will look up to and be inspired by. The project includes a real handmade prototype of the jacket, poster advertisements, website mockup of the online store, and wireframe of the UX/UI app development.
