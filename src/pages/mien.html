Mien Magazine, A Love Letter to LA

Mien Magazine is a conceptual lifestyle magazine that features style, fashion, art, design, and photography. This is a special edition dedicated to Los Angeles. The project showcases graphic and illustration heavy spread that entails the love and hate relationship with the one and only, Los Angeles. Photos used are taken by Gian Dionisi.

Make sure to flip through the book as pages with your cursor.
