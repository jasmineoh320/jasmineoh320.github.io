Sometimes Here, Sometimes Not

Sometimes Here, Sometimes Not (c.2016) studies the beauty of minimalism typography alone delivers. The message centers around the interchangeable effect the immigration issues have on the minorities and the non-minorities. The white text against the white background represents the frequent ignorance of an issue that is so close and tangeable to many affected.

The letters are handcut in bristol paper with foam glued on the back.
